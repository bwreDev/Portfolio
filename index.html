Colton Bonner

Hello, my name is Colton Bonner, I am a recent graduate of Thinkful's Engineering Flex Bootcamp and full-stack software developer trying to make his mark on the industry.
I have experience building applications using JavaScript, Node.js, Express, PostgreSQL, React.js, HTML, and CSS.
Along with helpful tools for testing like Jest, Mocha, Chai, Nodemon, and Morgan.
I have used several tools in my time as a developer, such as VSCode, Git, Github, npm, Postman, and DBeaver.

Ever since I was young, technology has always fascinated me, whether it was a new gadget or exciting breakthroughs in science and computing, I always knew that I wanted to be using the latest and most advanced tech available. Today this enthusiasm has brought me into the world of web development and programming.

My introduction into the developer mindset and world has been incredibly stimulating. I am beyond excited to meet and work with other software developers so that I can learn as much as possible, and so far I have been flooded with resources, tips, strategies, and an overwhelming amount of welcoming attitudes. This cemented in me very early on in my path to becoming a developer, that this is what I am and will be passionate about as a career.

In my spare time, I enjoy live streaming games and side projects as a twitch.tv affiliate and spending time with my newborn son.
I enjoy solving problems and my attention to detail has helped me increase efficiency and productivity in several industries from food service to construction and currently, AutoCAD drafting.
